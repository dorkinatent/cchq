## Design Context

### Users
Developers who run multiple Claude Code CLI sessions simultaneously across different projects. They work long hours, often late at night, switching between 4-6+ active sessions. They want a command center that feels like a natural extension of their workflow — not a flashy app, but a reliable tool they leave open all day.

### Brand Personality
Grounded, utilitarian, warm. Like a well-worn leather tool belt — not trying to impress, just built to work.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Default theme: Fossil** — warm stone tones, amber accents, dark backgrounds with organic warmth. Avoids the cold blue-gray developer tool cliché.
- **Available themes:** Midnight (deep indigo), Arctic (clean light), Terminal (green phosphor retro)
- **Anti-references:** Generic dark mode dashboards, neon-on-black developer tools, anything that screams "AI made this"
- **Typography:** Functional but with character. Not monospace-as-personality.

### Design Principles
1. **Warm, not cold** — tinted neutrals, amber over blue, earth tones over steel
2. **Dense, not sparse** — information-rich for power users, not card-heavy marketing layouts
3. **Quiet confidence** — no flashy animations, no gradients, no glow effects. Let the content breathe.
4. **Distinct but not distracting** — each theme should have a clear identity without fighting the content
